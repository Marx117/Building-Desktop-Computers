What are these computer parts?

There are many parts that make up a desktop computer. And they all contribute to a greater whole. However, each part individually is only good for one function. Think of it as the different lobes of the brain...

For instance, the Occipital lobe could be equated to the Graphics Card or GPU. Both serve as a part that is in charge of vision. For memory, the Hard Drive and RAM sticks both serve as long term and short term memory that the Temporal Lobe is in charge of. Peripherals such as CD Drives act as mediums in which information is store, much like books when we read and get information from something.
